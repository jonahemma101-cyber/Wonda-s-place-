Our Story

Crafted with
love, served
with pride.

Wonda's Place was born from a simple belief — that good food should make you feel at home. Tucked in the vibrant Agwan Zogele Junction of Tunga Maje, we've become the neighbourhood's most beloved dining destination.

Every dish is prepared fresh, seasoned with care, and served with the warmth that has made us a household name across Abuja's FCT communities. Whether you're stopping by for a quick plate or celebrating life's big moments, Wonda's Place is where flavour meets heart.
